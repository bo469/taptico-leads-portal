What We'd Build

The 90-day plan Haven needs

30

Days 1–30 · Foundation & Visibility

Audit and fix all Google Business Profiles across Haven's locations. Establish baseline local SEO rankings for "Columbus HVAC," "Columbus plumbing," "Columbus electrician" and top service-area keywords. Fix citation inconsistencies. Launch review generation program (the fastest ROI move in home services). Set up call tracking so we know which channels are driving calls today.

60

Days 31–60 · Demand Generation

Launch targeted Google Local Service Ads (LSA) for HVAC, plumbing, and electrical. Build location-specific landing pages. Stand up a seasonal demand calendar (HVAC peaks spring and fall — we need campaigns ready before the season, not during it). Begin content strategy targeting local homeowners with service-specific educational content.

90

Days 61–90 · Scale & Handoff

Email/SMS re-engagement campaign for Haven's existing customer base (fastest revenue recovery play in home services). Full analytics dashboard built and running.
