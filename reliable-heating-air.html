The Opportunity

46 years of reputation. The marketing hasn't kept pace with the company.

Dan Jape founded Reliable in 1978 on a simple premise: honesty, integrity, fair pricing, and quality service. That premise built one of the most recognized home services brands in metro Atlanta — the #1 Trane dealer in the entire nation, 300+ team members, over 200 trucks on the road every day, and two thriving nonprofits (HEAT and Sunshine on a Ranney Day) that are woven into the fabric of Atlanta's communities.

That's not a company that needs to be sold on growth. It's a company that has earned its position and is ready to protect it — because the competitive landscape has shifted faster than any time in the company's 46-year history.

⚠️ The PE Threat Is Real and It's Here

Private equity completed a record number of HVAC acquisitions in 2024, with activity accelerating into 2025. Coolray, One Hour, ARS, and Comfort Systems are all PE-backed and running national marketing budgets in Reliable's exact service area. They don't win on quality or community roots. They win on digital visibility — and they're outspending every independent operator in Atlanta right now.

Reliable's family-owned story, community involvement, lifetime warranties, and "Comfort as a Service" financing product are genuine differentiators. The opportunity is making sure every Atlanta homeowner who searches for HVAC, plumbing, or electrical services finds Reliable first — and understands why Reliable is different before they call anyone else.

The Buying Signals

Four reasons right now is the moment to move.

🏢

PE Competitors Flooding Atlanta

Coolray (Wrench Group), One Hour Air (Authority Brands), and ARS are running aggressive digital and LSA campaigns in metro Atlanta. They're capturing demand that should be going to Reliable — purely through marketing spend, not service quality.

💳

"Comfort as a Service" Is Undermarketed

In-house financing with no-charge repairs, maintenance, and filters is a remarkable offer. Most Atlanta homeowners have never heard of it. This product alone could drive a significant increase in long-term customer LTV with the right campaign.

⚡

Plumbing + Electrical Expansion Underexposed

Reliable added licensed plumbers and electricians in 2010. Fourteen years later, most Atlanta homeowners still think of Reliable as an HVAC company. A targeted multi-service awareness campaign is sitting on the table uncashed.

❤️

Community Story = Retention Engine

HEAT and Sunshine on a Ranney Day are remarkable stories that differentiate Reliable from every PE-backed competitor. When homeowners know their service fee helps low-income families stay warm in winter, loyalty goes up. This story isn't being told at scale.

The math on PE marketing spend: A PE-backed home services platform with 20 markets spends $2M–$5M/year on digital marketing in Atlanta alone. That's Google LSA dominance, retargeting at scale, and review velocity programs that push 4.8-star averages across thousands of properties. Reliable's family-owned story wins on trust. But trust requires visibility. Without the marketing to match, the best brand loses to the loudest one.
The 90-Day Plan

Three phases. Built to protect and grow Reliable's position in Atlanta.

1

Days 1–30: Digital Dominance Foundation

Google Local Service Ads audit and optimization across all service categories (HVAC, plumbing, electrical) — targeting every zip code Reliable services. Google Business Profile expansion: separate optimized profiles for HVAC, plumbing, and electrical to capture category-specific searches. Review velocity program: systematic process to turn Reliable's 200+ daily service calls into a 5-star review engine on Google, Yelp, and Nextdoor. Goal: outrank PE-backed competitors for "AC repair near me," "plumber Atlanta," and "electrician Kennesaw" within 60 days.

2

Days 31–60: "Comfort as a Service" Campaign + Multi-Service Awareness

Dedicated campaign for Comfort as a Service: targeted to homeowners 35-65 in Reliable's service area, emphasizing the "no-charge repairs forever" positioning. A/B tested creative showing the lifetime warranty math vs. competitors. Plumbing and electrical awareness campaign: "Did you know Reliable also does plumbing?" retargeting to existing HVAC customers — the highest-conversion audience Reliable already owns. Nextdoor advertising expansion: Reliable already has strong neighborhood presence; paid amplification turns that into systematic new customer acquisition across all service lines.

3

Days 61–90: Community Story at Scale + Retention Engine

HEAT and Sunshine on a Ranney Day content series: video and social content that tells the community impact story in a way that drives emotional connection and sharing. Email nurture sequence for Reliable's existing customer base: seasonal maintenance reminders, Comfort as a Service upsell, and HEAT donation matching campaigns that turn customers into advocates. Loyalty program launch: turn Reliable's strongest customers into a systematic referral engine. Dan Jape founder story content: the 46-year journey, the family business values, and why "If it's not us, it isn't Reliable" means something different when it comes from the founder himself.
