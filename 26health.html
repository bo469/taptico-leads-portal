The Situation

What's happening at 26Health right now

On March 13, 2026, 26Health — a comprehensive, inclusive healthcare organization based in Orlando, FL — announced the acquisition of Optimal Integrative Care, a respected Atlanta medical practice at 3193 Howell Mill Road NW in West Midtown/Buckhead. The practice specializes in infectious disease, internal medicine, and primary care.

26Health CEO Latrice Stewart: "Our shared commitment to compassionate, comprehensive care makes this a natural partnership. We look forward to building on the strong foundation that Optimal has established while expanding access to care in Atlanta and beyond."

What this means in plain terms: 26Health is now in Atlanta, and almost nobody in Atlanta knows it. The practice is keeping the Optimal Integrative Care name, patients are being told nothing will change — but the parent organization that owns and operates the clinic is completely unknown in this market.

The core problem: 26Health has a powerful, mission-driven brand built around inclusive care, HIV prevention, and health equity — exactly what Atlanta's LGBTQ+ community and underserved patients need. But without local brand presence, that mission doesn't reach the people it's meant to serve.

What We'd Build

The 90-day Atlanta launch plan

30

Days 1–30 · Digital Foundation & Retention

Establish 26Health's Atlanta digital presence: Google Business Profile for the West Midtown practice, optimized for both the Optimal Integrative Care brand and 26Health parent branding. Critical SEO setup for "LGBTQ doctor Atlanta," "HIV specialist Atlanta Midtown," "primary care West Midtown," and infectious disease keywords. Audit and optimize existing Optimal social accounts (Instagram @oic.atl, Facebook). Launch patient retention communications — existing Optimal patients need to feel reassured and welcomed by their new parent organization.

60

Days 31–60 · Community Brand Launch

PR pitch to Atlanta Business Chronicle, Atlanta Journal-Constitution, Creative Loafing, and local LGBTQ+ media outlets about 26Health's mission-driven expansion into Atlanta. Launch community partnership outreach to Atlanta LGBTQ+ organizations: Lost-N-Found Youth, Lost-N-Found, AID Atlanta, and Positive Impact Health Centers. Build 26Health's Atlanta-specific content program: patient stories, provider spotlights, health equity content tailored to the Atlanta market. Review management program to build Healthgrades and Google review velocity.

90

Days 61–90 · New Patient Acquisition

Launch targeted paid campaigns for new patient acquisition — Google Local Ads for primary care, Instagram ads for LGBTQ+ health services in Atlanta. Referral network development with Atlanta-area primary care physicians, social workers, and community health workers who refer to infectious disease and integrative care specialists. Full analytics dashboard and campaign reporting. Complete playbook for 26Health's communications team to maintain the program independently.
