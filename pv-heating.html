What We'd Build

The 90-day plan PV needs

30

Days 1–30 · Foundation & Visibility

Audit and optimize Google Business Profile for both HVAC and plumbing categories. Establish baseline local SEO rankings for "Atlanta HVAC," "Atlanta plumbing," and top service-area ZIP codes. Fix citation inconsistencies across directories. Launch review generation program (fastest ROI move in home services — you already have the 4.9-star rating, let's amplify it). Set up call tracking to know exactly which channels are driving calls today.

60

Days 31–60 · Demand Generation

Launch Google Local Service Ads (LSA) for both HVAC and plumbing — the verified badge that pushes PV to the absolute top of local search. Build plumbing-specific landing pages optimized for Atlanta service areas. Stand up a seasonal demand calendar so your spring AC campaigns are live before homeowners start searching, not after. Begin content targeting energy rebates, heat pump guides, and water heater topics PV already owns expertise in.

90

Days 61–90 · Activate the Member List

Email and SMS re-engagement campaign to PV's 5,000+ Priority Plan members — the fastest and highest-ROI marketing move available to any home services company. A single well-timed seasonal campaign to your existing customer base typically outperforms any paid acquisition channel. Plus: full analytics dashboard, competitive rank tracking, and a playbook for PV's team to maintain momentum independently if they choose.
